# Autism Central — User Manual

A calm, private app for parents to track their child's therapy journey —
sessions, daily moments, and milestones — with optional AI help.

**Live app:** https://autism-central.in
**Works on:** any phone or computer browser. Best as a Home-Screen app on iPhone/Android.

---

## 1. Getting started

### Install it on your phone (recommended)
**iPhone (Safari):** open https://autism-central.in → tap the **Share** icon →
**Add to Home Screen**. Open it from the new icon — it now runs full-screen like
a real app, works offline, and can show reminders.

**Android (Chrome):** open the site → menu **⋮** → **Add to Home screen**.

### First launch
A short **guided tour** runs automatically the first time, pointing out each part
of the app. You can replay it anytime from **⚙️ Settings → Help → Replay tour**.

### Set up your child's profile
Tap the **⚙️ gear** (top-right) → **Child profile → Set up**. Add a nickname
(used everywhere instead of "your child"), an optional photo, date of birth, and
which therapies they attend. Everything is stored privately on your device.

### Your data & privacy
- By default, all data lives **only on your device** — nothing is uploaded.
- Optional **cloud sync** (Settings → Account & sync) lets you sign in to back up
  and sync across devices, and unlocks the AI features.
- You can **export a backup file** anytime (Settings → Backup & restore).

## 2. The four tabs

### 🏠 Home
Your daily dashboard:
- **Today's sessions reminder** — a gentle nudge if a session is scheduled today
  or this week.
- **Attendance** — a ring showing attended / missed / upcoming across your
  institute therapy plans.
- **Daily streak** — how many days in a row you've done the at-home activities.
- **Latest milestone** — your most recent celebrated win.
- **How are *you* doing?** — a quick parent mood check-in (your wellbeing matters
  too; tough weeks surface supportive resources).

### 🗓️ Sessions
Track therapy plans and their sessions. Two sub-tabs:

**Sessions**
- Add a plan with the **+** button (OT, Speech, ABA, PT, or custom).
- Each plan shows a progress ring and attended/missed/remaining counts.
- **Upcoming sessions** card — a 7-day strip + list of anything you've scheduled.
- Open a plan to see its session-by-session timeline. Tap any session to:
  - Mark it **Attended** or **Missed**
  - For attended: record **how it went** (mood) + notes + attach documents/photos
  - For missed: add a **reason** (unwell, travel, etc.)
  - **Schedule** a future session with a planned date
- A quick **+/−** stepper lets you set how many sessions were done before you
  started using the app.

**Progress**
- **✨ AI recap** — turns your logged data into a warm summary (see Best Features).
- **Sessions delivered** bar chart and **mood trend** line chart over time.
- **Wins logged** count and a **milestone timeline**.
- **Export report (PDF)** — a clean, shareable report for your therapy team.

### ✅ Daily
Log the day in seconds (each day takes ~20 seconds):
- **How was [name] today?** — a 5-point mood selector.
- **A moment worth remembering** — capture a win (optional).
- **Anything to note?** — jot a concern (optional). You can add it to your
  therapist list, or tap **💡 Get gentle ideas** for supportive suggestions.
- **Quick log** — sleep, eating, medication, home exercise.
- **Sensory triggers** — tag anything that was tough (loud sounds, crowds…).
- **Home activities** — your daily checklist, including the AI-generated plan.
- Use the **date strip** at the top to log a previous day you missed.

### 📚 Resources
Curated, parent-friendly resources and videos by area (OT, Speech, ABA) and for
your own wellbeing. Add your own links with **+ Add resource**.

## 3. Best features

These are the things most worth using:

### ✨ AI weekly recap (Progress tab)
Tap **Write my recap** and the app reads everything you logged for the period —
daily moods, wins, concerns, sleep/eating, session attendance, and milestones —
and writes:
- a **warm recap for you**, highlighting what went well and gentle patterns;
- a few **highlights**;
- a tidy **"summary to share with the team"** you can read out at the next visit.
*(Requires cloud sign-in.)*

### 💡 Gentle ideas for tough days (Daily tab)
Logged a hard moment ("meltdown at the supermarket")? Tap **Get gentle ideas**
for 2–4 concrete, non-clinical things to try at home, plus one thing worth
mentioning to your therapist — added to your list with a tap. *(Requires sign-in.)*

### 🤖 Therapist-plan → home routine (Daily tab)
Upload a photo of your therapist's weekly plan (or paste the text) and the app
builds a **7-day, ~15-minute daily routine** with a helpful video for each day.
Check off steps as you go. *(Requires sign-in.)*

### 📅 Session scheduling & reminders
Give upcoming sessions a date and they appear in the **Upcoming** strip and as a
gentle **today/this-week reminder** on Home.

### 🌟 Milestones you can keep
Celebrate any win, add a photo, and **share a beautiful milestone card**. Delete
with one tap (with an undo if you change your mind).

### 📄 One-tap PDF report
The exported report gathers attendance, moods, milestones, and your therapist
notes into a clean document — great for reviews, school, or new providers.

### 🔒 Private by default, yours forever
Local-first storage, optional cloud sync, and a full export backup mean your data
is never locked in.

## 4. Tips

- **Log little and often.** A daily mood tap is enough to make the trends and AI
  recap useful.
- **Schedule sessions** as soon as you book them, so reminders do the remembering.
- **Back up monthly** (Settings → Export backup), especially if you don't use
  cloud sync.
- **Add to Home Screen on iPhone** — required for reminders to work.
- **Replay the tour** anytime from Settings → Help if you want a refresher.

---

## 5. Frequently asked

**Do I need an account?** No. The app works fully offline with no account. An
account only adds cloud backup/sync and the AI features.

**Is my child's data shared?** No. It stays on your device unless you turn on
cloud sync (then it's in your private account). AI features send only the
relevant text to generate a response and use no clinical/diagnostic language.

**Is the AI giving medical advice?** No. Everything is framed as supportive ideas
to review *with* your therapy team — never a diagnosis or medical advice.

**I changed phones.** Export a backup on the old phone (Settings → Export backup)
and import it on the new one, or sign in to cloud sync on both.
